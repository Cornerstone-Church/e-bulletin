  • Cornerstone Equip

    March 2nd | 9:00am - 12:00pm

    Everyone who is currently serving or interested in serving is invited to the Cornerstone Equip Leadership Track. In this session we will be addressing topics that will help us know how we as servant leaders of Jesus here at Cornerstone can help overcome hatred and division which is so prevalent in our nation today. One of our Special guests that day will be Dareth Ly, missionary to Cambodia, who will share his own story about growing up in a war torn and hate filled nation.

  • Resurrection Choir

    April 19th - 21st
    Rehersals: Every Tuesday at 6:45pm

    Share your musical talent with us this Resurrection Season! We are looking for singers ages 14 & up to ministers to the congregation with the choir for the Resurrection Season. No auditions are required. Rehearsals will be held in the choir room, behind the sanctuary, starting on March 12th. You are required to attend at least 4 rehearsals to participate. Register to participate at cag.org/events.

  • NC Missions Trip

    Cornerstone will be taking two trips to New Bern, NC to help with rebuilding homes that were damaged by Hurricane Florence. We still have several spots open for March 31-April 6. Open to men and women. The cost is $100 per person. If interested in more info, please email George at connections@cag.org.
