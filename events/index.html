  • Annual Members Meeting

    March 10 | 5:30pm

    Join us as we celebrate all the tremendous blessings from God in 2018 and look forward to the exciting plans for the future! Members are encouraged to attend, and attendees are also welcome. Childcare will be provided for children from age 1-5th grade. Please sign up at cag.org/events for childcare by Wednesday, March 7.

  • Pretty Pretty Outreach

    April 6th | 10:00am - 12:00pm

    Join us in showing God’s love to women in our local community. The event is free to the community and will include haircuts, manicures, makeovers, refreshments, and special giveaways. We invite ladies & men age 14 & older to sign up to serve at cag.org/events.

  • Resurrection Choir

    April 19th - 21st
    Rehersals: Every Tuesday at 6:45pm

    Share your musical talent with us this Resurrection Season! We are looking for singers ages 14 & up to minister to the congregation with the choir for the Resurrection Season, April 19 - 21, 2019. No auditions are required. Rehearsals are every Tuesday at 6:45pm in the choir room, behind the sanctuary, starting on March 12th. Participants are required to attend at least 4 rehearsals to be part of the choir. Register to participate at cag.org/events.

  • JOY Matinee

    March 16th | 1:00pm - 4:00pm

    If you are 50 or over, please join Just Older Youth on Saturday, March 16th from 1-4 pm to watch the movie “The Passion of the Christ” in Room L105. Signup online at cag.org/events and bring a snack to share.

  • Youth Camp

    Students grades 6th-12th are encouraged to join us for this year’s Youth Camp June 17-21. This week includes awesome activities, team games, and powerful nightly services at the Potomac Pine Creek Camp in Gore, VA. Registration forms and a $100 deposit are due by Sunday, March 31st. Register at cag.org/events.

  • Spring Men's Softball

    All men are invited to play softball on Tuesday nights starting in March. If interested, contact Jason Amos at connections@cag.org.
