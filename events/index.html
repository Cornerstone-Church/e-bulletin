    April 12th-14th | 7:00pm - 9:00pm

    Join us and bring a friend for an immersive and interactive experience of the power of the Resurrection. Step into a modern-day interpretation of Jerusalem as you are guided through the events that led up to Christ’s Resurrection.

    Have a dramatic flair? We are looking for dynamic people who love to interact with others to serve as cast, guides, and set crew. Rehearsals will be on Mondays and Thursdays at 6:30pm starting on March 18th. Roles will be assigned at the first rehearsal. Register to serve at cag.org/events.

    April 16th | 10:00am - 12:00pm

    Join us in showing God’s love to women in our local community. The event is free to the community and will include haircuts, manicures, makeovers, refreshments, and special giveaways. We invite ladies & men age 14 & older to sign up to serve online & attend the info meeting on March 24th after both services in the Community Room.

  • Summer Camps

    KIDS: July 8th-12th
    YOUTH: June 17th-21st

    ALIVE Kids and Fusion Youth Ministries are excited for this year’s summer camps. Kids Camp is July 8-12 for students 8 years old through 5th grade. Youth Camp is June 17-21 for students in 6th-12th grade. These week-long camps include awesome activities, team games, and powerful services at the Potomac Pine Creek Camp in Gore, VA. A non-refundable $100 deposit is due by Sunday, March 31. You do not want your students to miss this, so sign up now at cag.org/events.
